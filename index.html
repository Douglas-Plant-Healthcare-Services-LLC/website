Disease & Pest Management Tree Services in Rumson

Insects and diseases prey on distressed trees, especially when they lack proper nutrients, light, or water. Urban stresses like drought, construction, and pollution often weaken trees, leaving them open to attack.

At Douglas Plant Healthcare, our ISA Certified Arborists provide professional disease and pest management to diagnose problems and restore health before it’s too late. We identify whether stress is caused by disease, chewing or boring insects, or other environmental issues—and build the right treatment plan for lasting recovery.
